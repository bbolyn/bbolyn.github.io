Dear Data

"Dear Data" is a project where two designers, Giorgia Lupi and Stefanie Posavec, sent each other postcards every week for a year. Each postcard had a hand-drawn data visualization based on things from their daily lives, like how often they laughed or how many doors they opened. This project showed how personal data can be turned into art. Learn more about the project here.

For this assignment, I explored the "Dear Data" website, looked at the different visualizations, and made my own postcard based on my own data. I tracked how frequently I drank anything and for what reason i drank it.
