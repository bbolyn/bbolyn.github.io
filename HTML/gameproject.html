Artist's Statement

For my final Twine project, I created Toxic Gym, a game about chaotic gym culture and social media pressure. It mixes humor and serious choices with different outcomes depending on your stats.

I used ChatGPT to help generate ideas and make custom images for every passage. This helped bring the game to life visually. I DID NOT use AI to write the code, I did all of that on my own by learning Twine macros and variable systems.

The most rewarding part was getting everything to work together. Writing, logic, and images. It felt like a real project coming to life.

One frustrating part was trying to make the layout and images behave on every page. But I learned a lot about HTML, CSS, and how Twine works.
